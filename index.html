I am a Data Scientist with industry and academic experience, and a PhD from Nanyang Technological University. My work across research and industry focuses on applying state-of-the-art AI models to solve high-impact, real-world problems. Recent examples include:

  • Product recommendation in wealth management: Led the development of a machine learning recommender for bancassurance products, improving relationship manager productivity by 5× and generating over $20M in additional revenue within five months.
  • Agentic AI for data analysis: Designed workflows that combine large language models with reliable mathematical tools to process large volumes of semi-structured data, enabling scalable insight extraction from natural language.
  • AI for wireless communication in autonomous systems: Led applied research in collaboration with Continental, addressing complex challenges in autonomous vehicle communication, with results published in top-tier international journals and conferences.

I received my PhD in Computer Science from Nanyang Technological University, and my MEng in Mechanical Engineering from Imperial College London. I am particularly interested in collaborations on applied machine learning and real-world decision-making.
